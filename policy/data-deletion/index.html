How to delete your data permanently from Qffer

In accordance with GDPR compliance, Qffer provides an option to delete your account and all personal information right from the application itself.

You can follow the listed steps in case you need to delete the account and personal information. Please note, all this information will then be lost irrevocably and you will need to create fresh account in order to access Qffer again.

  1. Go to My account page

  2. Click on the “Delete Account” button.

  3. Enter “Delete my account” in the text box and press “Yes, delete my account” button.
